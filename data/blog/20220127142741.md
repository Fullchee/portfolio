### Check if a date is within the fiscal year

```sql
-- if fiscal year starts in Feb (instead of Jan)
SELECT *
FROM table
WHERE DATE_PART('year', date_column_name - '1 month'::INTERVAL) =
      DATE_PART('year', CURRENT_DATE::date - '1 month'::INTERVAL)
```
